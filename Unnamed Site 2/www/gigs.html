The violin, a sadly neglected blues instrument, was integral to the early development of the genre. Mlle Chat Noir and Doc Bowling met on stage once upon a time and have formed an intimate musical partnership that brings together the black cat's haunting fiddle with The Doc's original lyrics. Joined by Simon Minney/Melissa Rodrigues-Tull on bass and Louie Watson on cajon, look forward to a night of unconventional blues.
